From Out of Town?

Accommodations

We've reserved a group of rooms at Woodcliff Hotel at a special discounted rate. Please contact us for information on how to obtain a reservation. Woodcliff provides a free shuttle to and from the Rochester Airport by request.

Airports

The Greater Rochester International Airport (ROC) is the closest airport to the wedding venues. Next closest is the Buffalo Niagara International Airport (BUF) which is about an hour and 20 minute drive from Rochester.

Things to See

  • Corning Museum of Glass, the world's largest museum of glass
  • Finger Lakes Wine Tours and Trails. Upstate New York is famous for its wineries.
  • George Eastman House, founder of Kodak, and museum of photography and film
  • Lake Ontario or Canandaigua or Seneca or Cayuga or any of these
  • Niagara Falls, needs no introduction
  • Public Market, a huge local farmer's market
  • Rochester Institute of Technology RIT, where we met!
  • Rochester Red Wings baseball, minor league baseball at its finest
  • Strong National Museum of Play, aka the Toy Hall of Fame
  • Toronto, a fine Canadian city, home of the Hockey Hall of Fame

Food

  • Abbott's Frozen Custard, authentic custard, perfect for the occasional sunny day
  • Anchor Bar, the birthplace of the Buffalo wing
  • DiBella's Old Fashioned Submarines, a glorious sandwich experience
  • Dinosaur Barbeque, as featured on Food Network
  • Java's Cafe, it's always important to know where you can get a warm drink in Rochester.
  • Nick Tahou Hots, home of the Garbage Plate
  • Pizza Plant, bread + cheese deliciousness
  • Wegmans, the best supermarket ever. Really.
